FAQ

Quick Answers

When do new episodes drop? +

New episodes drop every Thursday at 8AM CAT. Subscribe on Spotify or Apple Podcasts so you never miss one.

Can I suggest a topic or question? +

Absolutely — use the form above or DM us on Instagram. The best listener questions make it into our episodes, sometimes with a shoutout!

Do you accept guest speakers? +

Yes! We love bringing fresh voices onto the show. Send us a message with your name, what you do, and what you'd like to talk about. We'll be in touch.

How do brand partnerships work? +

We work with brands whose values align with ours — authentic, community-first, and relevant to our audience. Reach out via email and we'll send you our media kit.
