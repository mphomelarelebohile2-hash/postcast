The Show

Why We Started

Subliminal started as a voice note. Palesa and Relebohole were in the middle of one of their usual long phone calls — the kind that goes from funny to deep without warning — when they thought: why isn't anyone talking about this properly?

So they grabbed a mic, found a quiet corner, and started recording. That was Episode 001. The conversations haven't stopped since.

Subliminal is for everyone who's tired of surface-level content — the young, the curious, the builders, the dreamers, and the people who just want to feel less alone in what they're going through.
