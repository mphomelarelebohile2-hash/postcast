"Soft Life or Hard Work? We Settle It."

📅 10 June 2025 ⏱ 48 minutes 🎙 Palesa & Relebohole

Is the soft life a genuine lifestyle choice — or a Gen Z cope? This week Palesa and Relebohole finally sit down to settle the debate everyone's been having on the timeline. They bring receipts, personal stories, and a lot of strong opinions.

They cover everything from hustle culture burnout to what it actually takes to build something, whether rest is resistance or a trap, and why women especially are told they can't want both ease and ambition at the same time.

What We Cover

  1. What "soft life" actually means vs. what social media sells you
  2. The burnout epidemic hitting young professionals in Southern Africa
  3. Can you be ambitious AND rest? We argue both sides
  4. Social media, comparison culture, and keeping up appearances
  5. What Palesa and Rele personally do to protect their peace while still grinding
  6. Listener questions: your takes on the soft life debate
